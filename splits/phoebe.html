Elite Four Phoebe

  • Without a boosting nature, Surf is a damage range on the first Dusclops. Go for the Hydro Pump, which will KO it if you have a neutral nature.
  • Surf the 2 Banettes and Sableye. You don't even get damage ranges in the the worst case scenario.
  • The second Dusclops is bulkier; it's a damage range with Hydro Pump even in the best of cases. If you've been doing well with KOs recently, go for the OHKO, as it's around a 50% shot. The Sitrus berry tends to act as a healing deterrent anyway.
  • Phoebe's fight is particularly PP draining, so use your Max Elixir after the fight. You can use it before the fight if you're confident you'll only need to use one Hydro Pump against her Dusclops. This is the case if you go for the damage range against the first Dusclops, which you can do if your SpA is on the upper end of a neutral nature.
