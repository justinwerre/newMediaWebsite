Champion Steven

  • Surf is a damage range on Skarmory at low IVs, but in those cases, Hydro Pump gets it. If you think you can, go for the Surf, though. You'll want to use Hydro Pump later.
  • Aggron, Claydol, and Armaldo are weak to Surf and get OHKOed without question.
  • For Cradily, if your SpA is good, go for the risky Hydro Pump OHKO. If not, go for the double Ice Beam on Cradily in hope for a freeze or crit.
  • Provided your SpA is not garbage, you should always Hydro Pump the Metagross. The odds are favourable and if it doesnt kill his sitrus will bring him out of healing range and you can kill with Surf.
  • If you have an X Special left over for whatever reason, you can take the safe way out and just use it on the Skarmory and sweep everything with Surf.
