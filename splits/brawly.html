Once you leave the gym, Head to the herb shop (The shop May walks out of).

Shop 4

This isn't the Pokemart, but the Herb Shop next to the Gym.

  • You should buy 4 heal powders and spend the rest on Energy Roots.
  • You can sell Overheat if you're worried about the amount of Energy Roots you have.

Switch Marshtomp back to slot 1 and Teleport back to Mauville. Use the center for the PP restore before heading back south.

Don't take cycling road down on the way back; Its faster to go below. Use a super repel and equip soft sand (only equip soft sand if you are going to Mudshot Brawly's Machop (Damage Range) - otherwise equip your Chesto Berry here). Rush through Slateport and down the beach and talk to Mr. Briney again. When you get back to Dewford, remember that you need to fight Brawly to unlock Norman. If you fought Laura during your first visit to Dewford, you're ready to fight Brawly, otherwise Laura goes down to 1 Mud Shot.

Leader Brawly

You've delayed him, so now you're stupidly overleveled. Mud Shot both his Pokemon to maximise your chance of finishing the fight in two turns, though Machop is a damage range even at high Attack levels and Makuhita can also be Strengthed with those same high Attack levels. If you're feeling unlucky, double Water Gun the Machop and Mud Shot the Makuhita.
