Elite Four Drake

  • Ice Beam Shelgon (his Rock Tomb is annoying), then use a Single X Speed on Altaria, and Ice beam everything in your path. You should have no problem with OHKOs. The worst Altaria can do is paralyze you with Dragonbreath, but you have plenty of options to heal that by now.
  • If you have a +Speed nature and a very high IV, you can skip this fight's X Speed (look for 118 Speed at Level 50 in the Glacia fight, which is a very tough mark to achieve but still possible for the speediest Kyogres)
