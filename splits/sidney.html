Leave the gym and do the following:

  • Fly to Mossdeep
  • Max Repel
  • Equip Mystic Water to Kyogre
  • Surf to Victory Road.

Navigating Victory Road without Flash is a pain, but the road is pretty linear in nature, and you only have to fight a few trainers with no spinners to worry about. Use your Max Repels when needed:

Cool Trainer Hope

Ice Beam. You'll level up here, and finally be able to get hard numbers on those Kyogre's Stats if you didn't look already.

Cool Trainer Shannon

Claydol can't deal with the sheer power of Surf

Cool Trainer Julia

2x Surf here.

Cool Trainer Edgar

Ice beam. None of these fights have any room for error. You just win.

Pkmn Trainer Wally

  • Ice beam on Altaria and Roselia, Surf on Magneton and Delcatty. None of these KOs will be a problem. Surf should also take out Gardevoir handily, use Hydro Pump if you're worried about your special attack
  • Keep in mind that Gardevoir has 99 Speed, so checking your level 47 Stats helps here, though you only need 91+ to outspeed it due to the Speed badge boost Wattson's badge gave you.

Time to enter Ever Grande City. Don't even go to the shop; march straight to the League Guards and into the challenge. This might seem crazy, but Kyogre dominates the Elite Four so hard that Full Restores are pretty much unnecessary.

Elite 4 Sidney

  • The Surf KO on Mightyena is guaranteed.
  • A neutral nature guarantees the KOs on Absol and Cacturne with surf and ice beam, respectively.
  • Thunderbolt Sharpedo away. If you don't have Thunderbolt, either use a single Hydro Pump or 2 Shock Waves depending on how good your SpA is.
  • Shiftry is a damage range with Ice Beam even at generous SpA levels. The only way to ensure the KO is to have a boosting nature. Other than that, just hope for the KO, Ice Beam is still your best bet.
  • The Sharpedo and Shiftry will probably be faster than you. If you outspeed the Shiftry, you have a good IV, and if you outspeed the Sharpedo, you have a boosting nature.
